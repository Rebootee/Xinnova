## Sony: The Process King

- **Double-layer transistor pixel** + Gen6 DBI 3D stacking (12 years of mass production)
- **14,335 patents**, averaging 127 citations each
- Process/manufacturing score: **10/10** — no competitor comes close
- LOFIC: IMX908 (STARVIS 3) entered mass production in March 2026
- **Risk**: Apple's in-house CIS (Samsung fabs) threatens Sony's largest customer

## Samsung: The Shrinkage Champion

- **0.5µm ISOCELL HP5** — world's smallest mass-produced pixel
- Most complete 200MP lineup (HP5/HP9/HP2/HP1)
- Hybrid bonding: **0 years of mass production** vs Sony's 12 years
- LOFIC: 2 years behind, still in R&D

## OmniVision / Will Semiconductor: The HDR Leader

- **TheiaCel**: LOFIC+DCG fusion — single-exposure HDR 130-140dB, industry best
- OV50X: 1-inch, 110dB, 12.5MP@180fps — flagship choice for Huawei and Xiaomi
- 2024 revenue: ¥25.73B (+22.4%), net profit ¥3.32B (**+498%**)
- **Risk**: Fabless — 22nm TSMC dependency is a geopolitical vulnerability

## SmartSens: The Growth King

- **LOFIC 3.0**: Fastest iteration cycle (1.0→2.0→3.0, 3 generations in 3 years)
- SCC90XS: **World's first 200MP+LOFIC sensor** (0.61µm, 105dB, 4K@240fps)
- 2025 revenue growth: **+51%**, net profit growth: **+155%**
- SFCPixel-2: Read noise 0.7e- — class-leading
