<h1>About Xinnova</h1>

<p><strong>Xinnova</strong> is my personal publishing platform for CIS technology research and semiconductor capital markets analysis.</p>

<p>I'm a CIS (CMOS Image Sensor) engineer with deep expertise in pixel architecture, process technology, and imaging system design. This blog is where I connect the dots between what I know in the lab and what I see in the market.</p>

<p>The name combines <em>Xin</em> (信 — truth, trust) and <em>nova</em> (new) — truthful innovation, grounded in verified data and first-principles thinking.</p>
